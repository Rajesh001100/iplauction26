📜 AUCTION RULES & GUIDELINES

📉 WHY IS MY SCORE NEGATIVE?

Every franchise starts the auction at -140.0 Points because you begin with Strategic Penalties that must be cleared by building your squad:

  • ❌ Under-size Squad: -100 PTS
    (Cleared only when you reach 18 players)
  • ❌ Low Spend Alert: -100 PTS
    (Cleared when you spend ₹7,500L+)
  • ❌ Empty Roster: -40 PTS
    (-10 pts for each of the 4 role groups missing)
  • 💰 Budget Bonus: +100 PTS
    (Starting bonus for ₹10,000L remaining)

🔥 HOW TO OVERCOME:

Buy players! Each buy reduces the Budget Bonus, but as you reach 6 Batters, 6 Bowlers, 3 ARs, and 2 WKs, the -40 penalty disappears and turns into +60 Bonus Points. Reach 18 players and spend ₹7,500L to clear the big -200 gap!

🏆 THE "PSBE" WINNER'S SYSTEM

P

🗂️ CARD 1: PERFORMANCE (The "P")

  • ✅ Normalization: Scores are calculated Per Match. Modern stars can now compete with legends.
  • 🏏 Batters: Points for Runs/m, Avg (5x), S/R (1x), and Boundaries.
  • 🏐 Bowlers: Points for Wickets/m, Economy, and Strike Rate.
  • 🧤 Keepers: Performance points for Catches and Stumpings (normalized).
  • ⚡ Experience: Experience (100+ matches) adds a Logarithmic Stability Bonus.
  • ❌ Confidence: Small discount (< 15%) for players with < 10 matches due to volatility.
S

🗂️ CARD 2: STRATEGY (The "S")

Is your squad balanced?

  • 🌍 Magic 8: Get exactly 8 International players +30 PTS.
  • 👥 Roles: Need 6 Batters, 6 Bowlers, 3 All-rounders, 2 Keepers.
  • 🏆 Met All Req: Get +75 PTS for a perfectly balanced squad!
  • ⚠️ Strict 18: Must reach exactly 18 players to avoid the -100 PTS penalty.
B

🗂️ CARD 3: BUDGET (The "B")

Are you good with money?

  • 💰 Mandatory Spend: Spend at least 75% (₹7,500L).
  • 📉 Penalty: Spending < 75% = -100 PTS.
  • 💎 Efficiency: Saving money after the 75% limit gives bonus points (+1 pt per ₹100L saved).
E

🗂️ CARD 4: EXPERTISE (The "E")

  • 🌟 MVP Bonus: Any ₹14 Cr+ player gives +40 bonus pts for match-winning potential.
  • ⚡ Power Player: Batters with 150+ S/R or Bowlers with < 7.5 Econ earn +50 PTS.
  • 📈 Tiered Valuation: Players sold for ₹8 Cr - ₹13.9 Cr earn a +20 PTS quality bonus.
!

🗂️ CARD 5: WINNING TIPS

  • 💡 Don't Wait: Buy early to clear the -200 initial penalties quickly.
  • ⚖️ Overseas Lock: Aim for exactly 8 International players early to secure the +30 PT bonus.
  • 💰 Budget Trap: Don't leave too much money! Saving ₹1,000L gives only +10 pts, but a star player can give +100+ pts.

📝 EXAMPLE 1: PHIL SALT (BATTER)

Stats: 600 Runs, 20 Matches, 35 Avg, 165 S/R, 40x4s, 20x6s

  • 🔹 Exp Bonus: log(20m) * 150 ≈ +198
  • 🔹 Stability (Runs/m + Boundaries): ≈ +175
  • 🔹 Quality (Avg*5 + SR*1): ≈ +340
  • 🔹 Power Bonus (S/R > 150): +50
  • 🔥 TOTAL SCORE: (515 * 0.95 Conf) + 198 + 50 = 737.2 PTS

📝 EXAMPLE 2: JASPRIT BUMRAH (BOWLER)

Stats: 150 Wkts, 125 Matches, 6.8 Econ, 18.5 SR, 2800 Balls

  • 🔹 Exp Bonus: log(125m) * 150 ≈ +315
  • 🔹 Quality (Wkts/m + Econ): ≈ +328
  • 🔹 Strike Skill (Bowl SR < 24): ≈ +19
  • 🔹 Power Bonus (Econ < 7.5): +50
  • 🔥 TOTAL SCORE: 315 + 328 + 19 + 50 = 721.2 PTS
