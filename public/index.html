📜 AUCTION RULES & GUIDELINES

1. Squad Requirements

  • ✅ Minimum Squad: 18 players (Penalty: -50 pts if below)
  • ✅ Maximum Squad: 25 players per franchise
  • ✅ Overseas Limit: Max 8 international players

2. Ideal Composition (Scoring Bonus)

Build a balanced squad to earn maximum points:

  • 🏏 6+ Batters: +15 PTS
  • 🏐 6+ Bowlers: +15 PTS
  • ⚡ 3+ All-Rounders: +15 PTS
  • 🧤 2+ Wicketkeepers: +15 PTS
  • 🏆 Complete Squad Bonus: +25 PTS

3. Bidding Rules

  • 💰 Starting Budget: ₹10,000 Lakhs (₹100 Crore).
  • ⏱️ Timer: 15 seconds. Resets on every new bid.
  • ⚖️ Bid Increments: Use provided buttons (+10, +20, etc.).
  • 🚫 Elimination: Auctioneer can eliminate teams for misconduct.

4. Leaderboard Scoring

  • ⭐ Star Power: 1 point for every ₹10L spent on players.
  • 📏 Squad Size: 2 points for every player in your squad.
  • 📉 Balance: Sum of your Composition points (+/-).
  • ⚠️ Major Penalty: -50 pts for size < 18.
  • ℹ️ Why -90 PTS initially?
    Teams start with -50 (Below 18 players) + -40 (Penalty for missing all 4 role requirements) = -90 Points. Start bidding to earn points!

💡 Example Calculation

Scenario: 18 players, ₹2,000L total spent, All role requirements met.
• Star Power: 20 pts (2000 spent / 100)
• Squad Size: 36 pts (18 players × 2)
• Composition: +60 pts (all roles met)
• Bonus: +25 pts (Complete Squad Bonus)
• TOTAL RATING: 20 + 36 + 60 + 25 = 141.0
