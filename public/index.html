📜 AUCTION RULES & GUIDELINES

1. Squad Requirements

  • ✅ Minimum Squad: 18 players (Penalty: -50 pts if below)
  • ✅ Maximum Squad: 25 players per franchise
  • ✅ Overseas Limit: Max 8 international players

2. Ideal Composition (Scoring Bonus)

Build a balanced squad to earn maximum points:

  • 🏏 6+ Batters: +15 PTS
  • 🏐 6+ Bowlers: +15 PTS
  • ⚡ 3+ All-Rounders: +15 PTS
  • 🧤 2+ Wicketkeepers: +15 PTS
  • 🏆 Complete Squad Bonus: +25 PTS

3. Bidding Rules

  • 💰 Starting Budget: ₹10,000 Lakhs (₹100 Crore).
  • ⏱️ Timer: 15 seconds. Resets on every new bid.
  • 💸 Mandatory Spend: Each franchise must spend at least 75% (₹7,500L) of their budget.
  • ⚖️ Bid Increments: Use provided buttons (+10, +20, etc.).
  • 🚫 Elimination: Auctioneer can eliminate teams for misconduct.

4. Leaderboard Scoring (Strategic 2.0)

  • 🚀 Performance Rating: Comprehensive calculation: (Matches × 0.2) + (Runs / 100) + (Wickets × 0.5) + (Avg × 0.5) + (SR / 10) + (4s × 0.1) + (6s × 0.2) + (10 - Economy) + (Catches × 0.1) + (Stumpings × 0.2).
  • 🌍 Overseas Optimization: +30 PTS for exactly 8 OS players. -50 PTS if you exceed 8 OS.
  • 💎 Elite Player Bonus: +20 PTS for every player bought for ₹1000L+ (₹10 Cr+).
  • 🌟 Specialist Bonuses: +15 PTS each for Finishers (SR 150+), Anchors (Avg 40+), or Economical Bowlers (Econ < 7).
  • 📈 Budget Efficiency: 1 point for every ₹100L remaining in your budget.
  • 💰 Mandatory Spend: Must spend 75% (₹7,500L) of budget.
  • 🚩 Initial Score: Strategy starts at -140 PTS. Meet goals to climb up!
  • ⚠️ Major Penalty: -50 pts for size < 18 or Spend < 75%.

5. Understanding Score Breakdown (P | S | B | E)

P: Performance Score
REWARDS: Career stats (Quality & Experience)
Logic: (Exp×0.2) + (Runs/100) + (Wickets×0.5) + (SR/10) + (Avg×0.5) + (4s×0.1 + 6s×0.2) + (10-Econ) + (Ctch×0.1 + Stmp×0.2)
S: Strategy Score
REWARDS: Roster Balance & Squad Composition
Logic: Starts at -140 points (Deficit: -50 low spend, -50 size < 18, -40 missing roles).
• +15 per Role (6/6/3/2), +30 for 8 OS, +25 Complete Squad Bonus.
B: Budget Efficiency
REWARDS: Value-for-money bidding
Logic: 1 Point for every ₹100L (1 Cr) remaining in purse.
E: Elite & Specialist Bonuses
REWARDS: Superstars & high-impact specialists
Logic: +20 per ₹10Cr+ player, +15 for Finishers (SR 150+), Anchors (Avg 40+), or Eco Bowlers (Econ < 7).
